    • Hi I am Leonel Esguerra and I am

    Frontend Web Developer and UI/UX Designer



    I currently live here in Laguna, Philippines. I studied Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. For the past four years of studying Computer Science, I was interested in the field of designing and developing websites and system. I love to see designs and I love to convert my ideas into reality. I want to pursue my career in designs and development of websites and system and practice my skill in the industry.

    In my early experience as an On-The-Job Trainee when I am Senior High School and College and, I was involved in communicating with different people, answering calls, accomplishing tasks, working with the team, and work independently. In my experience as an On-The-Job Trainee at DOST-FPRDI, I was assigned a role as a Frontend Developer. I am responsible for designing and developing the user interface (UI) of the system and preparing technical documentation. Being an OJT, I was also responsible to assist other employees in terms of technical aspects, attending seminars, answering calls and preparing weekly accomplishment reports.

    Currently, as a UI/UX Designer at Daily Social Studio for nearly 6 months, I have had the opportunity to hone my skills in various areas of design. My primary focus has been on UX research, where I have gained valuable experience in conducting competitors analysis and creating user flows. In addition to that, I have also dabbled in game design, web design, app design, and WordPress design, which have provided me with a diverse range of design challenges and allowed me to expand my skillset. In my role, I have been involved in designing and prototyping various web and mobile applications, working closely with developers to ensure that the final product meets the needs of our users. I have also contributed to the development of our company's WordPress website, creating custom designs and implementing them using WordPress themes and plugins. Overall, working at Daily Social Studio has allowed me to gain practical experience in various aspects of design and has provided me with opportunities to work on exciting projects with a talented and dedicated team. I look forward to continuing to develop my skills as a UI/UX Designer and contributing to the success of the company.
