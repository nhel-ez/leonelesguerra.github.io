    • DOST-FPRDI OD-PS Document Management Information System

    DOST-FPRDI - Office of the Director - Planning Staff - Document Management Information System is a system project during the 300 hours on-the-job training. The system can store the incoming and outgoing documents, can search a particular documents and categorize the document based on the physical cabinet in the office.

    Color Exploration

    The prime color of the system is dark and white. It is helpful for the admin if they are not comfortable in the very light system. The system has a gray color that balanced the visual of the system.

    Typography

    Nunito is a well-balanced sans serif typeface superfamily. It is a default Jetstream font which is one of the plugins for Laravel Frameworks and it is usually used in the web system.

    This is the original Logo of DOST-FPRDI. The logo combination is from DOST and FPRDI when the time FPRDI was under by DOST.
